FAQ

Good to know before you park.

Does Curb replace local parking rules?

No. Curb is designed to help explain parking signs clearly, but drivers should always follow posted signs and local regulations.

Does Curb upload my scan photos?

No. Scan photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to Curb servers.

Why does Curb need my location?

Location is used only when you perform a scan, so Curb can understand which local parking rules may apply.

Why add a vehicle profile?

Some parking rules depend on vehicle type, resident permits, or disabled permits. A profile helps make the result more relevant.
