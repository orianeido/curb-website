Curb Parking App Launches Worldwide: Download Now in 11 Countries

July 7, 2026 · 3 min read · By Ido Oriane, Curb Founder
images/blog/curb-parking-launch-hero.webp

Curb is now live and available worldwide! We're excited to announce that Curb's scan-and-park experience is ready for drivers everywhere. Download the app today and get instant clarity on parking signs in over 11 countries. Take a photo of any parking sign and get a clear, instant answer: can you park here, is it paid, or is it restricted?

What's new

  • A camera-first scanning flow that reads parking signs in real time.
  • Clear result states — allowed, not allowed, or conditional — with the reasoning behind each answer.
  • Time-aware logic that understands hourly and weekday restrictions.
  • An optional vehicle profile so results can account for permits and vehicle type.

Why we're building this

Parking signs are often confusing — especially when you're in a new city, in a rush, or trying to decode small print about hours and exceptions. Curb is designed to turn that confusion into a simple, confident decision in seconds, without uploading your scan photos anywhere. Everything happens on your device.

Our goal is simple: never guess parking again.

Coverage

Curb is now available across 11 countries including Israel, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, Netherlands, Spain, UK, and more. Our coverage continues to expand as we add support for new regions and cities. Whether you're a resident or a traveler, Curb helps you park with confidence wherever you are.

Available now

Get Curb on the App Store.

Download Curb and start scanning parking signs today.
