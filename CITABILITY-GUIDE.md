
**Optimized (STRONG - 160 total words, ~53 per step):**

**Step 1: Scan Parking Sign**
```
Point your camera at any parking sign and let Curb capture the image. Our photo recognition technology works in all lighting conditions—bright daylight, shadows, even nighttime street lighting. Curb automatically extracts all text, numbers, symbols, and color codes from the sign. The entire scan process takes less than 2 seconds.
```

**Step 2: Analyze Parking Rules**
```

```
